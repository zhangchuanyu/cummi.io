Why Choose Our Restaurant

Our story

A collection of young people's dreams has created Cumin & Pepper, a new Chinese restaurant that combines tradition and innovation. We believe that foods have direct powerful impact on our mood, and we are hoping our foods can make your day.

Our cuisine

In Cumin & Pepper, here are spice-flavoured, charcoal-fired sizzling BBQ, steam pots with a mellow fragrance, fried rice and noodles that full of Chinese flavours. With the absorption of essencial traditional Chinese food, consolidation of healthy green food, to create a new concept of the Chinese restaurant in Ottawa.
